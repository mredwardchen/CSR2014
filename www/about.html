Sets the Standard for Semiconductor Packaging Publications

Founded in 1997, Chip Scale Review is the leading international publication serving the semiconductor packaging industry today, bringing you the most up-to- date technology advancements in both contemporary and advanced assembly, test, and packaging processes and technologies for ICs, MEMS, RF/Wireless, optoelectronic, and emerging areas such as nanotechnology and LED packaging. From cover to cover, Chip Scale Review is packed with technology features, market trends, industry news, industry events, expert perspective, and international directories that can’t be found elsewhere.
